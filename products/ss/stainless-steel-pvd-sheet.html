PRODUCT DESCRIPTION


» SS PVD sheets are stainless steel sheets that have undergone a Physical Vapor Deposition process to enhance their surface properties.

» This advanced coating technique provides the sheets with a sleek, high-quality finish and improves their durability and resistance to corrosion.

» The primary applications of SS PVD sheets include architectural cladding, interior design elements, and decorative panels.

» SS PVD sheets are available in various colors and finishes, including gold, bronze, and black, allowing for versatile design possibilities.

» A common use is in modern building facades and interiors where aesthetic appeal and long-term performance are crucial.

» They are also used in high-end consumer products and appliances, enhancing both their appearance and durability.

» The PVD coating not only offers a visually appealing surface but also provides excellent scratch resistance and easy maintenance.

» SS PVD sheets are ideal for use in both indoor and outdoor environments, making them a popular choice for various applications in architecture and design.
